On-line Course Evaluation SPRING 2025 TRIMESTER

This is to inform all students of BSc. in CSE & BSc. in Data Science Program that their course/teacher evaluation for Spring 2025 Trimester (It is a mandatory task to be completed before Course Advising) will continue from June 23 – June 29, 2024 (Monday - Sunday).

All are advised to complete Course Evaluation in ucams.ulab.ac.bd within the deadline; otherwise you will not be able to continue (no chance to intake/registration) next trimester, Summer 2025. The students who admitted in Summer 2024 and who dropped current trimester of both the programs need not to complete course evaluation.

For more Details: https://cse.uiu.ac.bd/notice/on-line-course-evaluation-spring-2025-trimester/
